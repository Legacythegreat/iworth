Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is an Interactive Flat Panel?

An Interactive Flat Panel is a large touchscreen display that combines the functionality of a whiteboard, computer, and projector into one device. It's designed for collaborative work and presentations in educational and business environments.

2. Do you offer Delivery and Installation Services?

Yes, we provide professional delivery and installation services for all our products. Our certified technicians ensure proper setup and configuration of your equipment.

3. Do you do Power and Data Installations?

Yes, we offer comprehensive power and data installation services to support your AV and IT infrastructure needs.

4. Do you offer warranty?

Yes, all our products come with manufacturer warranties. We also offer extended warranty options and our own service guarantees for installations.

5. Do you have educational Set-Store?

Yes, we offer complete educational set solutions including interactive displays, projectors, and STEM kits tailored for classroom environments.

6. Do your screens come with a wall mount bracket?

Our Iworth model screens include complete wall mount brackets in the package. Please check alternate product specifications or contact us for details about specific models packaging.

7. When do I need a floor Stand?

Its ideal for boardroom setups where wall partitions are weak, alsoideal for training center where it can be moved from one room to another.

8. What is an OPS?

OPS (Open Pluggable Specification) is a complete computer module that can be inserted into the back of the Interactive Screens display all-in-one PCs, eliminating the need for external computers thus making the interactive screen multi-boot with both Windows and Android.
