Who We Are

With roots based in the IT, STEM, Power and AV world, iWorth Technologies focuses on technologies that impact our world. Through our Nairobi Kenya base we have a global reach through our alliance with partners and suppliers around the world. With the combination of industry leaders the company has the size, scope and technical talent to lead the industry into the future.

Our Mission

At iWorth Technologies, we partner with educators to create desired solutions. We provide cutting-edge technology solutions that help enhance the learning environment, stimulate creativity, encourage collaboration and make learning more engaging.

Our mission has always been to empower schools with the technologies and skills they need to prepare today's students for the world of tomorrow.

We are gradually enabling businesses and organizations to discover that the innovative, comprehensive solutions we offer improve outcomes, efficiency, and lead to organizational success.
